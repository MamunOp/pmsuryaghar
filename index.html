Expert Advice (FAQs)

Is subsidy guaranteed in Assam? ↓

Yes. As an authorized vendor, we only use ALMM approved modules. We guarantee the combined subsidy of ₹1,30,800 for 3kW+ systems as per National and Assam State guidelines.

How does solar work in Assam rainy season? ↓

Net Metering handles this. In summer, your panels produce excess energy. This excess is "stored" as credits with APDCL and adjusted during rainy months when generation is lower.
